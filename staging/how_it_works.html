Verify the truth.

When something fake causes real harm, clarity is the first step.

DefendMyRep was built to help people act quickly and confidently when deepfake sexual content appears. Our process is simple, private, and designed to put control back where it belongs—with you.

From verification to next steps, we guide you through what to do, what to avoid, and how to protect yourself or someone you love.

Proof comes first. Support follows. Action becomes possible.

Step 1: Verify the Content

Upload the image or video in question to a very secure external verification tool. This process is private, confidential, and designed to be completed quickly.

This technology analyzes whether the content was generated or manipulated using AI. Results are delivered with up to 99% accuracy.

Verification gives you clarity and evidence.

Step 2: Understand Your Options

Once you have proof, we help you understand what that proof means and how it can be used.

You decide how far to go. We support you either way.

How to talk to family members or trusted adults.

Ways to communicate with schools or employers.

How to document evidence.

How to prepare for legal or investigative steps.

Step 3: Take Back Control

Deepfake harm thrives on silence and confusion. Verification allows victims to reclaim their voice, reduce fear, and stop speculation.
