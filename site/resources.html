Education & Prevention

We provide educational materials for families, schools, and communities to help people recognize deepfake harm before it escalates.

We provide clear, accessible educational materials for families, schools, and communities.

  • Explain what deepfakes are in plain language
  • Help adults and teens recognize early warning signs
  • Reduce fear by replacing misinformation with clarity
  • Encourage proactive conversations rather than reactive panic
