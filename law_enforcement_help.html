 Help from Law Enforcement

  • Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) Lead federal agency for investigating cyberattacks.
  • National Crime Agency (NCA) UK-based organization focused on combating serious and organized crime.
  • Internet Crimes Against Children (ICAC) Task Force A national network under the DOJ’s Office of Juvenile Justice. Addresses online child sexual exploitation, including AI-generated abuse.
  • Virtual Global Taskforce (VGT) International law enforcement coalition tackling online child sexual abuse (includes deepfakes). Coordinated by U.S. Homeland Security Investigations and agencies like Europol and Interpol.
  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) U.S. Congress-established nonprofit. Manages the CyberTipline, where evidence of child exploitation (including deepfake content) is reported and routed to law enforcement.
  • Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Enforces the “Take It Down” Act — the new federal law criminalizing distribution of non-consensual intimate images, including deepfakes. Holds platforms accountable for takedown compliance.
  • Cyber Civil Rights Initiative (CCRI)  Offers victim support, monitoring of digital abuse trends, and advocacy for improved laws.
