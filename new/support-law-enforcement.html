Deepfake sexual exploitation presents unique investigative and procedural challenges. Many agencies lack dedicated cybercrime units or specialized AI expertise, yet are increasingly encountering these cases.

DefendMyRep supports law enforcement by providing:

  • AI‑based verification of suspected deepfake content
  • Documentation support suitable for investigative and legal use
  • Expert consultation on emerging AI threats
  • Guidance on victim‑centered response and communication

DefendMyRep does not replace existing authority or investigative processes. We act as a supplemental resource—helping agencies respond faster, reduce uncertainty, and better support victims.
